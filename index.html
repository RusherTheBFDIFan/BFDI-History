The winner was Firey, with runner ups Leafy and Bubble becaming second and third respectively. The second season, Battle for Dream Island Again, started in June 29, 2012 and ran until Aug 1, 2013. It was unfortunately canceled after 5 episodes. (BFDIA 5 had 5 parts, one being a game, specifically 5b.) The third season, dnalsI maerD roF elttaB, started in September 1st, 2016.
